# Product Requirements Document — NestJS SaaS

## Product name
Stockly

## Product Name
Stockly

## Repository
multi-tenant-inventory

## Vision
Provide a modular, scalable inventory and sales system for small retail stores, accessible via web and mobile.

## Target Users
Small retail stores with 1–5 employees:
- Clothing
- Hardware
- Kiosks
- Electronics
- Convenience stores

## Core Problem
Manual inventory management causes:
- Stock errors
- Poor sales visibility
- Difficult business analysis

## Success Metrics
- Register sale in <10s
- Maximum 3 steps to record a sale
- Mobile-first usability
- 20 active stores, >80% retention

## MVP Scope
- Inventory CRUD
- Product variants
- Register sales
- Register returns
- Sales history
- Dashboard metrics
- Low stock alerts

## Out of Scope
- Fiscal invoicing
- Payment integrations
- Multi-location stores
- CRM
- Ecommerce
- Advanced analytics# Product Requirements Document — NestJS SaaS
